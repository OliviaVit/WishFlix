Wishflix

About the Project

This project was created by Inès OUARGHI and Olivia VITTENET.

Wishflix is an interactive web application built around a TV series API. It allows you to discover and explore a wide range of TV shows with several features:

  • Main catalog: a home page displaying a catalog of series that you can sort by name, release year, or rating. You can also filter series by genre or search directly for a specific series.
  • Suggestions page: a dedicated secondary page where you can select your favorite genres and/or favorite series. Based on your choices, the app will generate 5 personalized movie recommendations.
  • Detailed series view: on any page, clicking on a series opens a detailed view showing seasons, episodes, synopsis, and more.

Our goal was to make discovering new series more fluid, interactive, and personalized to each user's taste.

How the Suggestions System Works

Our suggestion system is based on a smart filtering and scoring algorithm that combines the user’s favorite genres and selected series to deliver highly relevant recommendations.

  • Genre Selection: Users can select their favorite genres. These preferences are saved and used to filter the series catalog.
  • Series Favorites: Users can also pick their favorite known TV shows. The genres of these favorites are extracted to enrich the filtering process.
  • Smart Filtering: Each series is scored based on how many genres it shares with the selected preferences. The series that match the most criteria are prioritized.
  • Exclusion of Already Selected Shows: To avoid redundancy, series that have already been marked as favorites are excluded from the suggestions.
  • Summary Similarity Algorithm: We further refine suggestions by analyzing the similarity of show summaries: series that share similar keywords with the user’s favorites get a higher score.
  • Final Result: The 5 best matching series are displayed, offering a personalized discovery experience based on the user's individual taste.
