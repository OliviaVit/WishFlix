Wishflix is an interactive web application built around a TV series API. It allows you to discover and explore a wide range of TV shows with several features:

  • Main catalog: a home page displaying a catalog of series that you can sort by name, release year, or rating. You can also filter series by genre or search directly for a specific series.
  • Suggestions page: a dedicated secondary page where you can select your favorite genres and/or favorite series. Based on your choices, the app will generate 5 personalized movie recommendations.
  • Detailed series view: on any page, clicking on a series opens a detailed view showing seasons, episodes, synopsis, and more information.

Our goal was to make discovering new series more fluid, interactive, and personalized to each user's taste.
